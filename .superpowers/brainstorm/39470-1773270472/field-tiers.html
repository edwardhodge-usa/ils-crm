Which fields matter in the portal context?

The current Portal Access tab has 19+ fields. In the unified view, which tier does each field belong to?

Tier 1 — Always visible in the access list

These show inline on each person's row in the access list

Name

Contact's full name

Email

Login credential for portal

Company

From linked contact

Stage

Prospect / Lead / Client / Past Client / Partner

Date Added

When access was granted

Assignee

Who manages this relationship

Follow-Up Date

Next action date

Notes

Internal notes (preview)

Tier 2 — Detail panel only

These appear when you click a person to expand their details. Click any to promote to Tier 1 instead.

Project Budget

Currently: detail only. Click to promote.

Lead Source

Referral / Website / LinkedIn / etc.

Services Interested In

Strategy, Video, Design, etc.

Decision Maker

Who signs off

Expected Project Start

Timeline expectation

Position / Title

Contact's job title

Industry

Contact's industry

Phone Number

Direct phone

Website

Company or personal URL

Address

Physical address

How this works: Top section = fields visible at a glance in the access list. Bottom section = fields hidden until you click into a person's detail. I've pre-selected name, email, company, stage, and date added. Toggle anything you'd move between tiers, then let me know in the terminal.
